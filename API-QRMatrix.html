The _DARK and _LIGHT postfixed constans exist purely for convenience - their value is the same as QRMatrix::M_XXX | QRMatrix::IS_DARK and QRMatrix::M_XXX ^ QRMatrix::IS_DARK respectively, see QROutputInterface.
